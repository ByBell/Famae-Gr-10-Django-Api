Who we are

Did you know that, from 1996 to 2015, 28% of the U.S. population : almost 45 million of people in some years was exposed to unsafe drinking water ?

What’s in my water is a non-profit organization, who aims to make information more accessible for American citizens.

Water is a vital and precious resource, which is why we want access to clean drinking water for everyone.

Each quarter, we centralize data from all U.S. states, in order to analyze them and to inform the population about the drinking water contamination.

Everyone deserves the right to clean and safe drinking water, so let’s continue to fight for the American communities that need our help."
